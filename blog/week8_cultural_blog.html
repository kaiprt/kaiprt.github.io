Week 8: Chefs in the Kitchen

In this blog I discuss Fireside Chat with Shereef: Kitchen vs. Table. I find it best that you watch the first 20 minutes of video because it is very inspiring and you can relate more to my blog.

Based on the video, what's your take on the DBC experience?

Words cannot describe how excited I am to head to DBC. I feel like it was everything I dreamed of. I could directly relate to everything that Shereef discussed and I believe in the mindset that he has. Everything from the interviewing process to my last days in prep have been amazing and I can't believe how much I have learned. This is the best experience you can get outside of a career.

What is your impression of DBC?

I have a great impression (to the point that it supersedes university studies) of DBC and the people who run it.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I am directly engaged with the DBC culture. It has completely changed my life. For the first time I will be surrounded by an optimistic, creative, humble, and passionate group of individuals that are all working towards the same goal. I have dreamed for a moment like this. I feel that I have a true purpose.
