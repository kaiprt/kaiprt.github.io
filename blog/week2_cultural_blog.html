My most important value?
Leadership

Leadership is the most important value I have. My definition of leadership is the act of guiding others towards a positive outcome as a positive influence. The reason why I find it so important is because it has the most power to change something whether it's a work environment or a large society. I love helping others towards a set goal and making them the best they can be. It is my passion. I find it an opportunity not an obligation and I feel it is the best way I can contribute to society.
