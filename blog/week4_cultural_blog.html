Week 4: Dealing with Conflict

Discuss a time you had an emotional conflict with someone. What was it about? What happened?

A couple of months ago I had an argument with my mother about what I wanted to do with my life. I made a decision to stop school at my college and my mother was not happy. However, I wanted to leave because I felt that college was too slow paced in the "Information Age" and my mother looked at it from another prospective.

What was the source of conflict?

I had the best intentions when making my decision to go through and leave. I believe the culprits were poor communication, different values, and differing interests.

How did you handle the conflict?

Increased communication and presented a well crafted explanation that explained my thoughts, feelings, motives, and emotions towards the subject. I shed light on my value in order to give my mother a greater understanding.

Did you actions make the conflict better or worse?

Handling the conflict the way I did decreased the conflict.

If you could go back, what would you do differently, if anything?

I would have explained my thoughts and feelings before hand to avoid the conflict all together.

What did you learn from this experience?

That you should always stick to what you believe in and take everything with a grain of salt.
