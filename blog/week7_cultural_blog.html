Exercises to help feeling down at DevBootcamp

Personally, I am completely confident I will not feel "down" at DBC. I am excited to learn and meet new people in a city filled with life, interaction, and opportunity. I believe preparing and preventive maintenance prevent me from feeling this way. I use the following exercises to keep me positive, optimistic, and upbeat.

  • Physical Exercise- relieves stress and releases positive endorphins.
  • Yoga/Meditation- balances the mind and brings peace.
  • Dream- whenever you mind is idle DREAM it will embed your goals into your subconscious and the Law of Attraction will be put into work.
  • Motivate- if you feel like your drifting into a mental tailspin then listen to speeches and watch videos of people who succeed in the face of adversity. You will instantly realize you are more than capable of accomplishing what you desire.
  • Live through others success- this is a result from becoming a good leader and will allow you to see the positive in life.
  • Religion- everybody has different values and thoughts on this subject. Respect other's opinions and be true to yours!
