Apple.com

About

Apple Inc. creates some of the best consumer electronics on the market. Their products revolve around simplicity and are known for their high quality. Thankfully, Apple took this approach with their website which provides an extremely user friendly site by maintaining organization and optimization.

At first glance...

Apple.com has truly pioneered the web. It is a human factors masterpiece! At first glance you are immediately sucked into center screen where Apple places its most notable information. Below are for squares of subcontent that seemlessly match the width of the main content block. At a distance you might be drawn into how artistic the site's design is. Every image seems to be perfectly placed to provide positive emotions.

If I could describe Apple.com in 5 words:

  • Visual
  • Simple
  • Uplifting
  • Elegant
  • Artistic

Does Apple.com solve ANY problems?

If anything Apple.com provides an excellent example of usability, performance, and marketing with a perfect ratio of text to media content. It is quite easy moving from page to page. The navigation is set up in a tree like hiearchy keeping everything nice and neat.

Apple's content is:

  • Visual
  • Simple
  • Straight-forward
  • Balanced
  • Quality

Summary

Today, a website reflects the image of a company. After looking through this site there is no wonder why Apple is one of the most respected companies in the world.
