My Favorite Websites

In order to achieve a greater understanding of web development I have created a "Top 3" of my favorite websites! For each website I will discuss why I chose the site, a description, site usability, and a my honest opinion.

Top 3 Websites:

  1. www.apple.com
  2. www.lamborghini.com
  3. www.devbootcamp.com

Apple.com

About

Apple Inc. creates some of the best consumer electronics on the market. Their products revolve around simplicity and are known for their high quality. Thankfully, Apple took this approach with their website which provides an extremely user friendly experience by maintaining organization and optimization.

At first glance...

Apple.com has truly pioneered the web. It is a human factors masterpiece! At first glance you are immediately sucked into center screen where Apple places its most notable information. Below are four squares of sub-content that seamlessly match the width of the main content block. At a distance you might be drawn into how artistic the site's design is. Every image seems to be perfectly placed to provide positive emotions.

If I could describe Apple.com in 5 words:

  • Visual
  • Simple
  • Uplifting
  • Elegant
  • Artistic

Does Apple.com solve ANY problems?

If anything Apple.com provides an excellent example of usability, performance, and marketing with a perfect ratio of text to media content. Moving from page to page is quite easy and the navigation is set up in a tree like hierarchy keeping everything nice and neat.

Apple's content is:

  • Visual
  • Simple
  • Straight-forward
  • Balanced
  • Quality

Summary

Today, a website reflects the image of a company. After looking through this site there is no wonder why Apple is one of the most respected companies in the world.

Lamborghini.com

About

Automobili Lamborghini... need I say more? Lamborghini produces some of the fastest (most expensive) cars in the world.

At first glance...

Lamborghini's homepage is centered around one large content display. The design is set up to have its "hot" content flip through in a slide-show format which instantly draws the user to the center. The website navigation is on the top left with multiple tabs. When clicking on a tab a bar expands downward to reveal more specific content under the navigational tabs. On all pages the "Home" button is consistently in the top left of the screen along with language options providing great international accessibility.

If I could describe Lamborghini.com in 5 words:

  • Dynamic
  • Cinematic
  • Action-Packed
  • Informative
  • Freakin_Awesome!!! (I made that one word so it counts, okay? ;) )

Does Lamborghini.com solve ANY problems?

Absolutely not. This site isn't even for potential buyers. This site is more to generate attention than sales.

Lamborghini's content is:

  • Visual
  • Informative
  • Themed
  • Lengthy
  • Technical

Summary

Lamborghini certainly has one of the most dynamic auto company websites that provides an entire encyclopedia's worth of information about the company and its cars. It is well organized but lengthy and I seem to only visit the website for the visuals and interactive content. In my opinion, Lamborghini.com is one of the best car sites and the No. 1 exotic car site.

Devbootcamp.com

About

Dev Bootcamp is a learning institution that provides a hands-on, intense, and objective course that teaches students Ruby on Rails, HTML5/CSS, and many other advanced web technologies. I am currently enrolled in this school and I am more than excited to be here!

At first glance...

Dev Bootcamp's homepage is very simple, yet beautiful at the same time. It provides the user with a video taking up the first half of the page with navigation tabs on top and text content below. The site has a modern color scheme with a fluid feel.

If I could describe Devbootcamp.com in 5 words:

  • Beautiful
  • Bold
  • Informative
  • Modern
  • Expected

Does Devbootcamp.com solve ANY problems?

Devbootcamp.com doesn't solve many problems but the school provides a necessity to the tech world. This program allows you to have the opportunity to become a junior RAILS developer and become a great learner which greatly benefits society. The site also answers frequently asked questions, provides alumni statements, a gallery, and much more.

Devbootcamp's content is:

  • Informative
  • Trustworthy
  • Logical
  • Short
  • Notable

Summary

Dev Bootcamp has an amazing program and I believe it can be a game changer in the educational industry. The website is gorgeous and represents the company's image very well. The visual animations are so smooth that it feels natural. Furthermore, every element appears to be perfectly placed giving a sense of actually being in a classroom at Dev Bootcamp. Great work guys!
