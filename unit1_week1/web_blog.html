Apple.com

About

Apple Inc. creates some of the best consumer electronics on the market. Their products revolve around simplicity and are known for their high quality. Thankfully, Apple took this approach with their website and provided an extremely user friendly site by maintaining organization and optimization.

At first glance...

Apple.com has truly pioneered the web. It is a human factors masterpiece! At first glance you are immediately sucked into center screen where Apple places its most notable information. Below are for squares of subcontent that seemlessly match the width of the main content block. At a distance you might be drawn into how artistic the site's design is. Every image seems to be perfectly placed to provide positive emotions.

If I could describe Apple.com in 5 words:

  • Visual
  • Simple
  • Uplifting
  • Elegant
  • Artistic
